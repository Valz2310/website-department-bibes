The Department of Biomedical Informatics and Biomedical Engineering aims to improve diagnostic accuracy, develop innovative treatments and modernize the national healthcare delivery through tools like electronic health records, telemedicine, and artificial intelligence.
This helps in improving precision of diagnosis, health data management, treatment and rehabilitation of health problems.It currently holds two (2) Degree Programmes which are Medical Analysis and Informatics as well as Biomedical Engineering. Students who complete their degrees in these fields will acquire the essential knowledge and skills that are crutial in today's fast rapidly evolving technological and information age.
