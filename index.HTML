The Department of Biomedical Informatics and Biomedical Engineering aims to improve diagnostic accuracy, develop innovative treatments, and modernize national healthcare delivery through tools like electronic health records, telemedicine, and artificial intelligence.

This helps in improving precision of diagnosis, health data management, treatment, and rehabilitation of health problems. It currently holds two (2) Degree Programmes: Medical Analysis and Informatics and Biomedical Engineering. Students who complete their degrees in these fields will acquire the essential knowledge and skills crucial in today's fast, rapidly evolving technological and information age.
